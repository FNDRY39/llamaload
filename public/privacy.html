Privacy & Cookies Policy

Last updated: 10 January 2026

This Privacy & Cookies Policy explains how llamaload.com (“we”, “us” or “the Service”) collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the website and related services.

1. Who operates this service

llamaload.com is a small independent project. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach the creator at: hello@llamaload.com.

2. What data we collect

2.1. Information you provide directly

When you use the core tool, you provide website URLs that you want to generate mockups for. These URLs are processed by the service in order to take screenshots, but are not intended to be stored or associated with your identity on an ongoing basis.

2.2. Usage and technical data

Like most web services, basic technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • IP address and approximate region
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and actions taken (e.g. generating a mockup)
  • Date and time of requests

This information is used to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

2.3. Third-party services

If analytics or payment services (such as “Buy the creator a coffee”) are integrated, those providers may collect additional data in accordance with their own privacy policies. You should review those third-party policies when using their services.

3. How your data is used

We use information for purposes such as:

  • Operating and maintaining the core mockup generation features
  • Monitoring performance and reliability
  • Preventing abuse and troubleshooting issues
  • Understanding aggregate usage patterns to inform improvements to the Service

Data is not sold to third parties. If third-party analytics or payment tools are used, they may process information as independent controllers.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

The Service uses cookies and similar technologies to support core functionality and, with your consent, analytics via Google Analytics.

4.1. Essential cookies

Essential cookies are used to remember choices such as cookie consent. These are required for the site to function as expected and cannot usually be disabled without affecting the basic operation of the site.

4.2. Analytics cookies (Google Analytics)

With your consent, the Service uses Google Analytics to collect aggregated information about how visitors use llamaload.com. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on them
  • Buttons or features clicked
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device, browser, and operating system information

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to perform this measurement. Data collected through Google Analytics is processed by Google in accordance with Google’s own Privacy Policy .

Analytics cookies are only activated after you click “Accept” in the cookie banner. Until you give consent, Google Analytics is not loaded. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser.

4.3. Managing cookies

Your browser typically allows you to control cookies through its settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. Note that disabling cookies may impact how the Service functions. If you delete cookies, the cookie banner may reappear and you may be asked to provide your consent again.

5. Data sharing

Data may be shared with trusted third parties that help operate the Service, such as hosting providers or analytics vendors, under appropriate safeguards. Data may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Service, its users, or others.

6. Data retention

Technical logs and usage data are retained for as long as necessary to operate and improve the Service, and to meet legal or security requirements. URL inputs and generated screenshots are generally processed transiently to fulfill your request, and are not intended to be retained longer than necessary for that purpose.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, which can include the right to:

  • Request access to personal data held about you
  • Request correction or deletion of personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any rights or ask questions, contact hello@llamaload.com.

8. International transfers

The Service may be hosted or processed on servers located in other countries. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be processed in jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws than your own.

9. Third-party links

The Service may link to external sites (for example, “Buy the creator a coffee” or external documentation). This policy does not cover those external sites, and you should review their privacy policies separately.

10. Changes to this policy

This Privacy & Cookies Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the Service or applicable laws. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made.

11. Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact: hello@llamaload.com.
